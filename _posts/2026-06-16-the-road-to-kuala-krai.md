Every good ride starts with a bad decision. Mine was leaving KL at 5am on a Tuesday with a half-eaten nasi lemak and no backup plan.

The East Coast Highway opened up around Karak, and that's when the bike felt alive — that particular hum when the engine finds its rhythm and you stop counting kilometres.

Kuala Krai by midday. Ikan patin tempoyak at a kedai that had no menu, just an aunty who pointed at what was available. I ate everything she pointed at.

More roads to come.
